Trip to Turkey

In 2014, I was granted a scholarship to stay one year in Manchester, in the UK, studying Materials Science and Engineering. Being in Europe was great to travel to other countries, because they're so close, like an 1h flight! I got the chance to travel to one place I've always wanted to go: Turkey.
It's such a beautiful place!
I took this trip by myself and the itinerary was Istanbul -> Cappadocia -> Pamukkale -> Istanbul.
The whole trip lasted 10 days and it was wonderful!
Below we have a photo gallery with all my photos from this trip.
